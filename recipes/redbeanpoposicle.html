Red Bean Popsicle Recipe

Do you like having a nice Red Bean Popsicle to cool down during this hot summer weather in Singapore? Follow the following simple steps to make one at home!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz Dried Red Beans
  • 16 oz Water
  • 8 oz brown sugar
  • 8 oz milk
  • 6 oz coconut milk
  • 1.5 tbsp tapioca starch
  • 1.5 tbsp water

Steps

  • Prepare Red Beans
  • Cook the Beans
  • Blend with other ingredients
  • Pour into Moulds and freeze
  • Remove from mould and enjoy your popsicle!
