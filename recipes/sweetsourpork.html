Sweet Sour Pork

The Recipe to cook a mean Sweet Sour Pork!

Are you ready to find out how you can cook a mean sweet and sour pork? The dream of many kids and what a lot of young adults use it as a staple now!

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut and Marinate Pork
  2. Cut Vegetables and Pineapples
  3. Prepare Sauce
  4. Coat pork in Flour
