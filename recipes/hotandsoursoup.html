Hot and Sour Soup Recipe

Some times having that craving for something hot and sour just hits the right spot. Follow the steps to replicate a delicious hot and sour soup at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tomato
  • 2 oz Carrot
  • 2 oz Seafood Mushroom
  • 2 oz King Oyster Mushroom
  • 3 piece Shiitake Mushroom
  • 0.25 oz Dried Wood Ear Mushroom
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 oz Red Bell Pepper
  • 2 piece Green Onion
  • 2 piece Dried Chili Pepper
  • 14 oz Tofu(firm)
  • 5 cup water for soup base
  • 2 tbsp Vinegar
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 0.50 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp water
  • Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp corn oil
